About

The University of Johannesburg’s Metal Casting Technology Station (MCTS) is an initiative of the then [LN1] Department of Science and Technology (now Department of Science and Innovation - DSI) under the Technology Innovation Agency (TIA).
The MCTS operates under the Faculty of Engineering and the Built Environment in partnership with the Department of Metallurgy.

Product

The MCTS supports and assists the Metal Casting industry; Foundries, Suppliers and related industries, to improve the sectors innovation ability for increased competitiveness and sustainability, with the following FOCUS AREAS:
  • Physical Metallurgy
  • Moulding Technology
  • Foundry Technology
  • Support services.
